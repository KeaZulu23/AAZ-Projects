About

Building Excellence Since 2014

Founded by Mr. Jabulani Mfeka, Alwa and Zena Projects is a 100% black-owned South African company with 90% female and youth representation. Since our establishment in 2014, we have been committed to delivering exceptional services in general building, civil works construction, and the supply and delivery of protective clothing and cleaning materials. Our dedication to serving both rural and urban communities has positioned us as a trusted partner in the public sector, with a track record of successfully completed projects worth over R15 million.

Our Vision

To become South Africa’s premier construction company, recognized for quality, integrity, and innovation, while contributing to the country’s economic and social development.

Our Mission

  • Deliver top-quality services with superior craftsmanship and timely completion.
  • Expand our footprint across South Africa, impacting more communities.
  • Lead in the supply of protective clothing and cleaning materials, serving the public sector with reliability and excellence.

Our Values

  • Excellence: Consistently exceeding client expectations.
  • Sustainability: Reducing environmental impact through eco-friendly practices.
  • Empowerment: Investing in the professional growth and well-being of our workforce.
  • Integrity: Upholding honesty and transparency in all our operations.

What Sets Us Apart

With a dedicated team of experienced professionals, we combine technical expertise with a passion for creating impactful solutions. Our ability to integrate sustainability, innovation, and empowerment into our operations makes us a standout in the construction and supply sectors.
